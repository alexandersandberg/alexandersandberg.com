Around 2.5 years ago, I [joined Composer](/articles/joining-composer/) as their first iOS Engineer to help bring Composer to mobile. It’s been one of the most rewarding chapters of my career so far, and an absolute blast working alongside this team.

Today I’m excited to share some big news: **Composer is joining [SoFi](https://www.sofi.com)!**

![Composer by SoFi logo](https://cdn.alexandersandberg.com/articles/composer-by-sofi.png)

For anyone unfamiliar, [Composer](https://www.composer.trade) is an automated investing platform that lets you build, test, and run trading strategies—all without writing a single line of code.

I’ll be joining the SoFi Invest team, starting by leading the mobile integration of Composer into SoFi. I’m looking forward to bringing our intelligent trading tools into the hands of millions!
