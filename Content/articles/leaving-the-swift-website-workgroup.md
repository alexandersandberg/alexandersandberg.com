It has been just over 4 years since I [joined the Swift Website Workgroup](/articles/announcing-the-swift-website-workgroup/) to help guide the evolution of Swift.org.

Since then, I have been part of many workgroup discussions and changes to the website and its processes, including implementing features, reviewing PRs, updating content, helping out with the redesign, and authoring Swift release blog posts.

The time has come for me to focus on other things in life though, and I have decided to leave the workgroup.

I’m really grateful for my time in the workgroup, and it’s been an honor to serve the Swift community over these 4 years!

If you’re interested in filling my spot, you can learn more about the workgroup and how to apply on [the website workgroup page on Swift.org](https://www.swift.org/website-workgroup/).
